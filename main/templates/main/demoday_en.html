{% trans '5ο Thessaloniki Demo Day on 12 September 2019' %}

{% trans 'Open invitation to participate in the Thessaloniki Demo Day - Applications close on 25 August 2019' %}

{% blocktrans %} OK!Thess invites startup teams to meet investors from Greece and South-East Europe by applying to participate in the next Thessaloniki Demo Day, taking place on 12 September 2019, at OK!Thess. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans 'What is Thessaloniki Demo Day' %}

{% blocktrans %} Thessaloniki Demo Days are events organised by OK!Thess to promote networking between startups and investors of risk capital from Greece and abroad. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %} The previous Demo Day was held in May 2019 at OK!Thess and was the largest ever gathering of Venture Capital funds and business angel networks in Thessaloniki with the sole purpose of holding private meetings with groups of the startup ecosystem. 24 investors participated in more than 130 meetings with 36 startups from Greece and Cyprus. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans 'Who are the investors participating' %}

{% blocktrans %} At the 5th Demo Day the participants that are expected include the following active investors (VC and angel networks) from Greece and across South-East Europe:

  • Metavallon
  • Velocity Partners
  • Unifund
  • BigPi

  • Thermi Ventures

Ιnvestors are looking for early-stage opportunities across the spectrum: from hardware to software technology and services for markets in health, transportation, energy, commerce, manufacturing, agriculture, foodtech, fintech, tourism. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans 'Terms of participation for startups' %}

{% blocktrans %} The only prerequisite for the participation of a startup in the Thessaloniki Demo Day is to have reached a level of maturity and preparation sufficient for the founding team to present a complete investment proposal (the “ask”).
The proposing teams do not have to have participated in OK!Thess’ incubation and acceleration program. The invitation is open to any team from anywhere. It is also not obligatory for a team to present itself to investors for the first time. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans 'Process' %}

{% blocktrans %} Interested teams are invited to submit applications which are sent to investors for them to review. The investors will express their interest to meet specific teams in 1-1 meetings on Septemnber 12th. A schedule of meetings will then be made by OK!Thess, the teams involved will be informed and the booking will thus be confirmed. {% endblocktrans %}


{% trans 'Timetable' %}

  • 25/8: Deadline for submission of the startup summary (Apply here)
  • 4/9: Applicants are informed about the schedule of meetings
  • 12/9: Thessaloniki Demo Day
